GoBabyGo! Request a Car - Child Application Form

GoBabyGo! is a global program aimed to improving the lives of those with mobility impairments. We modify ride-on-cars to suit the unique mobility needs of each child, primarily at workshops all around the world!

Please answer the questions below so we can fit your child with an awesome GoBabyGo Car! You will need a measuring tape and your child nearby - the application requires measurements so we can fit them in the correct car. Any child with a mobility impairment is eligible to apply. There is no age restriction; however, the largest car we currently modify accommodates children around 4ft tall. This application is only the first step - it is not confirmation of eligibility to receive a car. If you have any questions, please email workshops@gobabyglobal.com. Thank you! :)
